ADTalks

Client

The Algemeen Dagblad is there for everyone. The term newspaper is perhaps somewhat outdated to describe the AD, as they also spread the news through other media. In fact, online, the AD reaches more than three million people a day.

Design problem

How can Algemeen Dagblad's offerings and the news needs of young people meet?

Context

This project is from early 2019.

Young people read news in conjunction with other activities and like it when the topics match their personal interests. 48% of young people say they regularly listen to podcasts in a Reuters survey.

Role

Concepter, User experience researcher, prototyper

Publications

As this project won in the Artificial Intelligence category, Marleen, a teammate, got to present our concept at the National AI Course.

It is very important to start a creative session with an energizer. This ensures that every team member is mentally present at the session with a fresh perspective.

5 Why's

During this assignment you will get to the root of the problem by asking yourself why your own problem is actually so. In this way you will get to the root of the problem which should make it easier to find a concrete solution.

Channel mapping

By applying this technique, the AD's channels will become more manageable. It can also provide insight into preferences and relevance to the target audience.

With this technique, you first analyze the data obtained from the interviews using a bottom-up approach. By organizing these properly, you get a good picture and see patterns emerge.

Earlier we had brainstormed with the team about how we envisioned the concept to make sure we were on the same page. While we were doing that, I came up with the idea of preparing a personal podcast every day based on the interests that user would indicate.

Deliver

ADTalks

ADTalks is a news platform where podcasts are in the spotlight. With ADTalks you can listen to your news whenever and wherever you want. Create your own podcast playlist and stay informed about the world around you!

ADTalks targets highly educated young people, ages 18 to 25. ADTalks allows young people to get away from the screen for a while, but still receive the information they need. With the help of AI, podcasts are broken down into short bites and categorized. These bites can be shared on Social Media.
