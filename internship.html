Internship

About the company

Easy LMS is an internationally growing company that specializes in educational technology. They want to provide the best user experience to customers, their employees or students. They strive every day to improve Easy LMS and provide great support to its customers.

Design problem

How can Easy LMS provide potential clients with an educational and easy onboarding process?

Publication

I wrote an article about my work during my internship. In it, I explain what we changed about the onboarding process through my research.

What I've learned

Focus

At the end of my internship, I work on a maximum of two tasks at a time.

Stakeholders

At the end of my internship, I actively involve stakeholders in the process.

Visual information

At the end of my internship, I will present my research in a visual manner.

Transferability

At the end of my internship, my products are transferable.

Planning

At the end of my internship, I will be able to plan a design process.

Responsibility

At the end of my internship, I will be able to substantiate my design decisions.

Data

At the end of my internship, I will be able to use quantitative data in the design process.

User testing

At the end of my internship, I perform proper user testing.

Results

During my internship, I worked primarily as a UX researcher. In doing so, I did a lot of research on the user and the product. I found it very interesting to be able to focus on this for six months and learn a lot about it.

I have found that I find it difficult to keep thinking from the user's perspective while constantly being in the client's environment. As a result, I am more likely to think from the company's perspective than if I am working with the team in a different location than the client.
