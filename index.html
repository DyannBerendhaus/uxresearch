Qualitative research

Allows gaining deep insights into users' attitudes, behaviors, and experiences. By using techniques such as interviews, user testing, and observational studies, I can gather detailed information that can guide the design process. This will help understand the context, as well as a user's motivations, preferences, and pain points. By leveraging the power of qualitative research, I can help you create products and services that are more user-centered and meet the needs of users more effectively.

Cross-checking

Enables a more complete picture of users' experiences, behaviors, and attitudes. Combining qualitative and quantitative data, such as database information or analytics, creates a better understanding of the user and their jobs to be done. This leads to more effective solutions. Quantitative data can provide a broader context for the qualitative findings, helping to validate or refute them.
